Explore

The goal of this project is to learn about how different communities react to different words and phrases. Here, you can select a subreddit, or subcommunity from the social media site Reddit.com and enter a word or phrase. Then, when you press submit (and wait a short bit), you will see a graph of the average score for comments that contain the entered word or phrase. You will also see how the score is affected by the comment being positive or negative!
