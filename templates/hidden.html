David Smith Capstone Project

  • This project utilizes 1.5 million comments scraped from Reddit.com.
  • Allows you to see how liked or disliked a person, object, word, or phrase is.
  • For example, a business may use this to see if a product is liked by a specific group.
  • Or a politician may use this to see in which commuinities they are liked.
  • I have also created a model that can be used to predict how well a comment is liked.
  • This is helpful for a group that wants to successfully reach many people.
